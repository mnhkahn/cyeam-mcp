# The Self-Cultivation of a Programmer

*The Self-Cultivation of a Programmer: Linking, Loading, and Libraries* (《程序员的自我修养：链接、装载与库》) is a Chinese technical book on low-level C program mechanics.

The subject read it in early 2013 and judged it a classic. It covers the principles of C compilation, linking, and runtime execution. The original motivation was to prepare for cross-platform compilation work, but the subject later noted that the knowledge went unused and was forgotten.

## Impact
Despite the material eventually fading from memory, the book reinforced the subject's respect for systems-level fundamentals.
