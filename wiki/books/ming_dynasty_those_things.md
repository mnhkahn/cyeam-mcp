# Ming Dynasty Those Things

*Ming Dynasty Those Things* (《明朝那些事儿》) is a Chinese historical narrative series about the Ming dynasty.

The subject read it throughout a six-month internship at IBM in 2013 and described it as the book that influenced them most that year. The author presents Ming history through a personal interpretive lens. After finishing the series, the subject expressed interest in reading the philosopher Wang Yangming's works.

## Impact
It replaced *Where Is My Hometown* as the most influential book of the year and deepened the subject's interest in Chinese history and philosophy.
