# Waves at the Peak

*Waves at the Peak* (《浪潮之巅》) is a book by Wu Jun that chronicles the rise and fall of major Silicon Valley technology companies.

The subject read it in 2013 and found it compelling. The book surveys the developmental histories of companies that shaped the technology industry.

## Impact
Reading it alongside *Beauty of Mathematics* gave the subject a well-rounded appreciation of Wu Jun's ability to explain both business history and technical science.
