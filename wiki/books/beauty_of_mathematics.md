# Beauty of Mathematics

*Beauty of Mathematics* (《数学之美》) is a popular science book by Wu Jun about the algorithms underlying natural language processing.

The subject read it in 2013 and was impressed by Wu Jun's ability to explain NLP algorithms in accessible terms, even to readers outside the field. The book's influence was strong enough that the subject considered revising their graduation thesis title around it, though a supervisor's intervention prevented the change. The subject noted that the book deserved multiple readings.

## Impact
The book demonstrated how complex technical ideas can be made approachable, leaving a lasting impression on the subject's view of technical writing.
